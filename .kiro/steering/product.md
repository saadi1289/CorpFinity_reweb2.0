## Product Overview

Corpfinity is a corporate wellness and productivity application built with Flutter. The app focuses on helping users improve their work-life balance through features related to:

- Productivity enhancement (5-minute focus sessions)
- Healthy lifestyle choices and nutrition
- Stress reduction and mental wellness
- Physical fitness tracking
- Better sleep habits
- Social connections

The app uses a modern, clean design with a teal accent color (#5FCCC4) and emphasizes user onboarding through authentication flows with social login options (Google, Facebook) and email-based registration.

Target platforms include mobile (iOS/Android), web, and desktop (Windows, macOS, Linux).
